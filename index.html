Non-negotiables
Sciatica protocol
If pain flares mid-walk: stop. Turn around. Walk easy back. Do not push through nerve pain — ever.

Positions that help: lying on your back with knees bent, or on your side in fetal position.

Positions to avoid: prolonged sitting on hard surfaces, forward bending with straight legs, sitting with legs crossed.

Heat vs. cold: gentle heat on the lower back reduces muscle tension. Ice if acutely inflamed.

Red flags — see a doctor if: you lose bowel/bladder control, foot drop, or numbness spreading.
Migraine awareness
Hydration is your #1 defense. Dehydration is the most common migraine trigger.

If a headache starts during a walk, stop, hydrate, find shade.

Alcohol + dehydration = migraine setup. On nights you drink, add an extra 16 oz of water before bed.
